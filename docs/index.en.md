# Applied Process Analysis And Modeling Laboratory

Welcome to the Applied Process Analysis And Modeling Laboratory. We focus on developing innovative solutions in computational mechanics and materials science.

## Research Areas

- **Computational Mechanics**: FEM, DEM, MPM
- **Granular Materials Modeling**: CT-based Particle Reconstruction, Shape Characterization, Contact Detection
- **Machine Learning Applications**: Deep Learning for Material Parameter Identification, Structural Health Monitoring
- **High-Performance Computing**: GPU Acceleration, Parallel Computing
- **Infrastructure Health Monitoring**: Computer Vision-based Structural Inspection and Assessment
