# Lab Members

## Principal Investigator

### Associate Professor Zhengshuo Lai
- Title: Associate Professor
- Research Areas: Computational Geomechanics, including Finite Element Method, Discrete Element Method, Material Point Method, Random Field, Computational Fluid Dynamics, and Multi-scale Methods; Advanced Numerical Algorithms and Software Development for Process Simulation Analysis and Infrastructure Disaster Prevention
- Office: School of Civil Engineering, Sun Yat-sen University
- Email: laizhengsh@mail.sysu.edu.cn

#### Education
- Ph.D. in Civil Engineering, Clemson University, USA, Dec 2018
- B.E. in Transportation Engineering, Sun Yat-sen University, Jun 2012

#### Professional Experience
- Mar 2023-Present: Associate Professor, School of Civil Engineering, Sun Yat-sen University
- Feb 2021-Feb 2023: Postdoctoral Fellow (Hong Kong Scholars Program), Department of Civil and Environmental Engineering, Hong Kong University of Science and Technology & School of Intelligent Engineering, Sun Yat-sen University
- Feb 2019-Feb 2021: Postdoctoral Fellow, School of Intelligent Engineering, Sun Yat-sen University
- Jun 2018-Dec 2018: Research Intern, Idaho National Laboratory, USA

#### Research Projects
- Hong Kong Scholars Program, Multi-scale Simulation of Granular Media, Feb 2021-Feb 2023
- National Natural Science Foundation of China (Youth Program), Macro-micro Mechanical Testing and Multi-scale DEM Modeling of Calcareous Sand Based on CT Images and Machine Learning, Jan 2020-Dec 2022
- China Postdoctoral Science Foundation, Discrete Element Method for Irregular Particles Based on Fourier Series, Jan 2020-Dec 2021
- University Basic Research Fund (Young Faculty Program), Intelligent Detection and Health Assessment of Transportation Infrastructure Based on Computer Vision, Jan 2020-Dec 2021

#### Awards and Honors
