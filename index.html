Zhengshou Lai, Ph.D.

Welcome! I am currently a postdoc researcher at the School of Intelligent Systems Engineering, Sun Yat-sen University. My general research interest is to explore and utilize the potential of computer vision and machine learning techniques in the field of Computational Geomechanics, such as detecting infrastructure defects, investigating material constituents and microstructures, generating realistic numerical samples, developing computational particle mechanics models, etc. A detailed Curriculum Vitae of me is listed in page About. My Sun Yat-sen University homepage (in Chinese) is here.
