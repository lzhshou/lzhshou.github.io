About the lab

The APAAM Lab is dedicated to computational geomechanics and multiscale numerical simulation, developing advanced computational methods and software tools for numerical modeling, uncertainty quantification, and data-driven computation of complex multiphysical processes.

Research Areas

Computational geomechanics, DEM, and multiphysics coupling—methods, software, and applications.

Intelligent Characterization

Deep learning image reconstruction, virtual particle generation, data-driven constitutive modeling

Irregular Particle DEM

SDF unified contact theory, full-process digitization, machine learning acceleration

Multiscale Fluid Coupling

Hybrid resolved CFD-DEM, phase-change fracture coupling, multiphase flow instability

Random Field & Reliability

Intelligent investigation optimization, rotated anisotropy, energy-evolution failure criterion
