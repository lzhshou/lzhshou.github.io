## Course Description

This graduate-level course covers frontier theories and methods in computational mechanics, including nonlinear finite elements, multiscale modeling, and high-performance computing. Students will develop numerical computation skills for solving complex engineering problems.

## Learning Objectives

1. Master theoretical foundations of nonlinear finite elements
2. Understand multiscale modeling methods
3. Develop high-performance computing program development skills
4. Conduct independent research in computational mechanics
