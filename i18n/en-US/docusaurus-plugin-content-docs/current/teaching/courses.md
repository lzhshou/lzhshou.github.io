# Courses

## Undergraduate Courses

### [Engineering Drawing & CAD](./engineering-drawing)

Fundamentals of engineering drawing and computer-aided design. Develops students' ability to draw and read engineering graphics.

---

### [Linear Algebra](./linear-algebra)

Matrix theory, vector spaces, linear transformations, and other fundamental mathematical tools for engineering computation.

---

## Graduate Courses

### [Advanced Computational Mechanics](./advanced-computational-mechanics)

Nonlinear finite elements, multiscale modeling, high-performance computing, and other frontier numerical methods.

---

### [Discrete Numerical Methods](./discrete-numerical-methods)

Discrete Element Method (DEM), granular material mechanics, and multiphase coupling algorithms.
