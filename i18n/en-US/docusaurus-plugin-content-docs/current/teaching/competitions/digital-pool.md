# Digital Pool Competition

## Overview

The "Hailutong Cup" Digital Pool Competition and Pioneer Intelligent Computing Special Session focuses on the interdisciplinary field of marine engineering and intelligent computing.

- **Organizer**: Hailutong and related institutions
- **Participants**: Sun Yat-sen University students
- **Format**: Numerical simulation competition

---

## 2025 1st Edition: KCS Hull CFD-DEM Coupled Simulation

### Research Content

The team conducted a numerical simulation study of ship-free-surface-particle multiphase flow using the **KCS (KRISO Container Ship) standard hull model** as the research object, based on the coupling of the Immersed Boundary Method (IBM) and the Discrete Element Method (DEM).

Using the Phynexis solver (interIBdem), the team simulated the motion response of the KCS hull model in a two-phase flow environment (water-air, surface tension coefficient 0.07 N/m), and introduced discrete particles through the DEM method to study the interaction between the ship and the particulate medium during navigation.
