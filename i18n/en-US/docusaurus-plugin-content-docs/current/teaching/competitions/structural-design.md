# Structural Design Competition

## Overview

The Structural Design Competition of the School of Civil Engineering is a school-level academic competition aimed at cultivating students' structural design and innovation capabilities, and deepening their understanding of structural mechanics principles.

- **Organizer**: School of Civil Engineering, Sun Yat-sen University
- **Participants**: Undergraduate students of the School of Civil Engineering
- **Format**: Structural design, fabrication, and load testing
