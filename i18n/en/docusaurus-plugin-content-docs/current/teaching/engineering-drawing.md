# Engineering Drawing & CAD

## Course Information

- **Course Type**: Undergraduate Foundation Course
- **Credits**: TBD
- **Semester**: TBD

## Course Description

This course covers fundamental theories and methods of engineering drawing and computer-aided design (CAD). Students will learn:

- Principles of orthographic projection
- Drawing and reading of engineering graphics
- Operation skills of AutoCAD and related software
- National standards and specifications for engineering drawing

## Learning Objectives

1. Master fundamental principles and methods of engineering drawing
2. Develop ability to draw and read engineering graphics
3. Proficiently use CAD software for engineering drawing
4. Cultivate engineering awareness and standardization mindset
