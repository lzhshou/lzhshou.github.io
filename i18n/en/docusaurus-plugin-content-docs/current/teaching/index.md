3. **English Proficiency**
   - Ability to read English literature

We welcome students with the following research interests:

- **Software Development**: Scientific computing software development

### Training Objectives

- **Theoretical Foundation**: Solid foundation in mathematics and mechanics

- **Innovative Thinking**: Ability to solve research problems independently
- **International Perspective**: Track international frontiers and participate in international exchanges
